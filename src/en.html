The annual campus party.

The annual campus party in Karlstad will be held in the month of may this year (2022). For the exact date, ask around.

Please respect the following guidelines:

  • Pick up your trash.
  • Do not destroy the propery of KBAB.
  • Treat police officers, security guards and attendees with respect. Always obey security guards commands while you are on thier assigned property, and always obey police officers commands at all time.
  • The consumption of alcohol or any other recreational substances are neither encouraged or is in any way mandatory for attending.
  • There is no need to traverse stairs.

This site only acknowledges and honors the tradition of The campus party. This is an unofficial event that is not formally organized by anyone.
