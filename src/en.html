The annual campus party.

The month of may, year 2022.

Most of the coronavirus related restrictions have been liften, click here to read the Swedish governments release (in english). The annual campus party in Karlstad will be held in may of 2022. For the exact date, ask around, your fraternity is a good start.

Please respect the following guidelines:

  • Pick up your trash.
  • Do not destroy the propery of KBAB.
  • Treat police officers, security guards and attendees with respect. Always obey security guards commands while you are on thier assigned property, and always obey police officers commands no matter what.
  • The consumption of alcohol or any other recreational substances are neither encouraged or is in any way mandatory for attending.
  • There is no need to traverse stairs.
  • If you are wearing an official fraternity clothing, like the iconic boiler suite (ovve), please contact your fraternity before attending. This is sometimes disencouraged by the board of some fraternities.

This site only acknowledges and honors the tradition of The campus party. This is an unofficial event that is not formally organized by anyone. If you need further assistance please contact your fraternity. Keep safe and have fun.
